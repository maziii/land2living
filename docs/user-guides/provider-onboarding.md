# Service Provider Onboarding Guide — Land2Living

**Who is this for:** Plumbers, electricians, builders, cleaners, gardeners, and other service providers who want to list on the Land2Living marketplace  
**Contact:** Your L2L founder contact on WhatsApp

---

## What is Land2Living for providers?

Land2Living connects you with verified residents in the Ndebele community who need your services. When you list on the platform:

- Residents can find you and request your services directly
- The platform handles payments and holds them securely until work is confirmed complete
- Your reputation builds up through verified ratings
- You get more repeat business from a trusted client base

---

## Requirements to join

You must:
- Operate within the Ndebele Royal Council area (geographic coverage)
- Provide a valid phone number (WhatsApp preferred)
- Provide your bank account details for payment disbursement
- Agree to the Land2Living Provider Terms

Optional but helpful:
- CIPC registration number (for business verification)
- VAT number

---

## The onboarding process

### Step 1: Contact us
WhatsApp your L2L founder contact to express interest. They will send you a registration link.

### Step 2: Register your business
On the registration form, provide:
- Business name
- Service categories (e.g. plumbing, electrical, repairs — you can select more than one)
- Geographic coverage (which areas of Ndebele you serve)
- Primary contact phone number (WhatsApp)
- Bank account details for payments

Your bank details are encrypted — no staff member can see the full account number after you enter it.

### Step 3: Verification
The L2L team will verify your registration. You will receive a WhatsApp message when your status changes to "Verified". Unverified providers can register but will not appear prominently in search results.

### Step 4: Start receiving bookings
Once verified, residents can find you on the app and book your services.

---

## How bookings work

1. A resident submits a booking request for your category
2. You receive a notification on WhatsApp
3. Log in to the web portal (`ndebele.land2living.co.za`) to view the booking
4. Submit your quote (the amount you will charge, in ZAR)
5. The resident accepts or declines
6. If accepted, you start the work on the agreed date
7. Mark the job as started in the portal
8. When done, mark it as complete
9. The resident confirms completion
10. Payment is released to your bank account within 1–2 business days

---

## The platform fee (take rate)

Land2Living charges a platform fee on each completed booking. This is deducted from the resident's payment before disbursement to you:

| Service type | Platform fee |
|---|---|
| Gardening, cleaning, security | 11% |
| Plumbing, electrical, repairs | 7% |
| Architecture | 6% |
| Bricklaying, fencing, borehole, building | 4% |

**Example:** You quote R5 000 for a plumbing job (7% fee). The resident pays R5 000. Land2Living keeps R350. You receive R4 650.

The fee is locked in at the time you submit the quote — it will not change after the resident accepts.

---

## Payment and escrow

For your first 3 jobs with any given resident, the platform holds the payment in escrow until both you and the resident confirm the work is complete. After 3 successful jobs, escrow is optional.

If a resident disputes the work:
- The payment is held until the council secretary resolves the dispute
- Document your work with photos — this protects you

---

## Rating and reputation

After each completed job, both you and the resident rate each other (1–5 stars). Your average rating is visible to all residents when they search for providers. High ratings lead to more bookings.

---

## Updating your bank details

Bank details are write-once for security reasons. If you need to update your account:
- Contact your L2L founder directly
- You will need to verify your identity before the change is made
