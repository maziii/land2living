# Council Secretary User Guide — Land2Living

**Role:** Council Secretary  
**Platform:** Web browser (Chrome recommended) at your council's L2L URL  
**Support:** Contact your L2L founder on WhatsApp for help

---

## Logging in

1. Go to your L2L URL in Chrome (e.g. `ndebele.land2living.co.za`)
2. Enter your email address and password
3. You will receive a one-time code on your phone — enter it to complete login
4. You are now on the Dashboard

**If you forget your password:** Click "Forgot password" on the login page and follow the email link.

---

## The Dashboard

The sidebar on the left has these sections:
- **Residents** — view and manage resident records
- **Stands** — view the land register
- **Applications** — review new land applications
- **Resales** — manage property resale listings and transfers
- **Services** — view service bookings in your council
- **Suppliers** — send quote requests to hardware and material suppliers
- **Audit** — view the full activity log

---

## Reviewing a land application

1. Click **Applications** in the sidebar
2. Find the application — use the status filter to show "Under review"
3. Click **View** to open the application
4. Review the attached documents (ID, supporting letters)
5. Choose one of:
   - **Approve** — the applicant will be notified and a PTO will be issued
   - **Defer** — for applications needing more information (add a note)
   - **Reject** — for applications that cannot proceed (add a reason)

The applicant receives a WhatsApp notification automatically when you take action.

---

## Issuing a PTO manually

PTOs are issued automatically when you approve an application. If you need to issue a PTO for an existing resident not going through the application process:

1. Go to **Stands** and find the stand
2. Click the stand to open it
3. Use **Assign resident** to link a resident to the stand
4. In the PTO section, click **Issue PTO**
5. Enter the issue date and confirm — a signed PDF will be generated

---

## Approving a resale listing

1. Click **Resales** in the sidebar
2. Find listings with status "Pending council approval"
3. Click **View** to open the listing
4. Review the listing details and the seller's PTO
5. Click **Approve listing** to make it live, or **Reject** with a reason

Once approved, the listing is visible to residents on the mobile app.

---

## Processing a resale transfer

When a buyer makes an offer and the seller accepts:

1. The listing status changes to "Under offer"
2. Open the listing in **Resales**
3. Review the accepted offer
4. Click **Initiate transfer** — this creates a payment link for the buyer
5. Once the buyer pays (you will see the status change to "Transfer pending"):
   - Verify the payment reference matches
   - Click **Approve completion** — this transfers the PTO to the buyer

---

## Managing service bookings

The **Services** section shows all bookings in your council. You cannot create bookings (residents do that on the app), but you can:

- Monitor which providers have bookings
- Resolve disputes — contact both parties and record the resolution
- View completed bookings and check ratings

---

## Sending a supplier quote request

When the council or a provider needs materials:

1. Click **Suppliers** in the sidebar
2. Click **New request**
3. Add the items you need (description, quantity, unit)
4. Select which suppliers to send the request to
5. Set the response deadline (default: 7 days)
6. Click **Create and dispatch** — suppliers are notified automatically

When responses come in, go to **Suppliers → [your request] → Compare** to see all quotes side by side. Click **Select** next to the best quote.

**If a supplier phones you with a quote** instead of replying digitally:
1. Open the quote request
2. Click **+ Add manual response**
3. Enter the details and click **Save response**

---

## Commission tracking

When a supplier fulfils an order:

1. Go to **Suppliers → Commission tracker**
2. Click **Record sale** (or it may appear automatically)
3. Enter the fulfilled amount
4. Click **Invoice** to mark that an invoice has been sent to the supplier
5. When the supplier pays the commission, click **Mark paid**
