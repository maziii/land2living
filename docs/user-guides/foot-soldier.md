# Foot Soldier User Guide — Land2Living

**Role:** Foot Soldier (field registration agent)  
**Device:** Android phone with Chrome browser  
**Language:** English (Ndebele and Sotho versions coming)

---

## Installing the app on your phone

1. Open **Chrome** on your Android phone
2. Go to: `ndebele.land2living.co.za`
3. Log in with your username and password
4. Chrome will show a message: **"Add Land2Living to Home Screen"** — tap it
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen like a normal app
6. Open it from your home screen from now on

**The app works offline.** You can register residents and stands even when you have no data signal. Everything will sync when you reconnect to the internet.

---

## Logging in

1. Open the Land2Living app
2. Enter your email and password
3. Your login stays active for 7 days — you won't need to log in every day

---

## Registering a new resident

This is your most common task. Do this when a resident asks to be added to the digital register.

**What you need:**
- The resident's full name
- Their ID number (green ID book or smart card)
- Their phone number (WhatsApp preferred)
- A photo of their face (for the record)
- A photo of their ID document

**Steps:**
1. Open the app and tap **Residents**
2. Tap the **+** button (bottom right)
3. Fill in the form:
   - Full name
   - ID number
   - Phone number
   - Date of birth
4. Tap **Add photo** to take a photo with your phone camera — take one of their face and one of their ID
5. Tap **Save**

The resident is now in the register. They will receive a WhatsApp confirmation.

**If you have no signal:** The form will save on your phone. It will sync to the system automatically when you get signal. You will see a small sync icon on the record until it syncs.

---

## Assigning a resident to a stand

After registering the resident, you need to link them to their stand.

1. Tap **Stands** in the menu
2. Find the stand (you can search by stand number or scroll the map)
3. Tap the stand to open it
4. Tap **Assign resident**
5. Search for the resident you just registered
6. Confirm the assignment

The council secretary will then issue a PTO from the web portal.

---

## Registering a stand that is not in the system yet

If a resident's stand is not in the system:

1. Tap **Stands**
2. Tap **+** to add a new stand
3. Enter the stand number
4. Drop a pin on the map to mark the location (walk to the stand if needed)
5. Enter the plot size if known
6. Tap **Save**

Then assign the resident to the stand as above.

---

## What to do if a resident refuses

Some residents may be suspicious of the registration process. Explain:

- "This is the Ndebele Royal Council's digital land register"
- "Your information is protected by law (POPIA)"
- "You will receive a digital copy of your PTO"
- "This does not change your occupancy rights — it protects them"

If they still refuse, do not force them. Note their stand number and report to the council secretary.

---

## What information NOT to collect

You should only collect what the form asks for. Do not:
- Write down ID numbers in your own notebook
- Take extra photos beyond what the form asks
- Share resident information with anyone other than through the app

This is required by law (POPIA).

---

## Checking your daily progress

Tap **My Activity** in the menu to see:
- How many residents you registered today
- How many stands you've visited
- Any records that are still waiting to sync

---

## Getting help

Call or WhatsApp your supervisor or the L2L team if:
- The app won't open
- A record won't sync after 24 hours
- You're not sure what to enter in a field
- A resident has a complaint

---

## Emergency: if you lose your phone

Call your supervisor immediately. They will lock your account so no one can use it.
